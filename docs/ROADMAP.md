# AI SAHELI Roadmap

**Project:** AI SAHELI
**Tagline:** Empowering Every Voice. Protecting Every Life.
**Status:** Draft v0.1
**Source of Truth:** GitHub repository

---

## Phase 1 – Voice-First Accessibility Companion

**Target:** 21-day MVP

### Objective

Enable elderly users, first-time smartphone users, and users with accessibility needs to operate common smartphone functions using simple Hindi voice commands.

### Core Features

* Wake-word detection
* Hindi voice command recognition
* Open WhatsApp
* Open Instagram
* Open Phone
* Open Camera
* Scroll up / down
* Back / Home navigation
* Call family member
* Basic SMS assistance
* Hindi voice feedback
* Android Accessibility Service integration
* Smartwatch simulator for demo flow

### Success Criteria

A user can perform basic smartphone actions without relying on touchscreen navigation.

---

## Phase 2 – Guardian Companion

**Target:** 21-day MVP alongside Phase 1 where feasible

### Objective

Provide basic emergency and daily safety support while preserving user independence.

### Core Features

* Emergency keyword detection, such as “Bachao” and “Madad”
* Guardian Mode
* Emergency countdown flow
* Emergency call trigger
* Distress sound prototype
* Fall detection prototype
* Medicine reminder
* Water reminder
* Simulated SOS escalation

### Success Criteria

AI SAHELI can demonstrate a basic emergency assistance workflow and daily reminder capability.

---

## Phase 3 – Family Circle

**Target:** After interview MVP

### Objective

Extend AI SAHELI from an individual assistant into a family care ecosystem.

### Planned Features

* Family companion app
* Circle of Care
* Multiple family/caregiver contacts
* Emergency alerts to family members
* Alert acknowledgement
* Escalation if no one responds
* Location sharing
* Caregiver dashboard
* Emergency history

### Success Criteria

Family members can receive, acknowledge, and respond to AI SAHELI emergency events.

---

## Phase 4 – Healthcare Companion

**Target:** Future

### Objective

Support long-term wellbeing and health-related assistance.

### Planned Features

* Health monitoring integrations
* Abnormal cough detection
* Stress indication prototype
* Emotion recognition prototype
* Wellness summaries
* Caregiver insights
* Wearable sensor integration

### Important Note

AI SAHELI will remain an assistive system and must not be positioned as a medical diagnosis tool.

---

## Phase 5 – AI Companion

**Target:** Future

### Objective

Evolve AI SAHELI into a personalized, multilingual AI companion for independent living.

### Planned Features

* Natural conversation
* Long-term memory
* Personalized assistance
* Regional language support
* Offline LLM exploration
* Context-aware assistance
* Smart home integration
* Family interaction support

---

## Current Execution Focus

For the first 21 days, the project will focus on:

1. Phase 1 – Voice-First Accessibility Companion
2. Phase 2 – Guardian Companion

Phase 3 and beyond will be designed for architectural compatibility but implemented after the interview MVP.

---

## Roadmap Principle

AI SAHELI will be built incrementally.

The MVP should remain small, useful, and demonstrable, while the architecture should remain flexible enough to support Family Circle, healthcare features, multilingual support, and wearable deployment in future phases.
